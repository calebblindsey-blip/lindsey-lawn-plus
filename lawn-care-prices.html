No Contracts. No Hidden Fees.

We don't require long-term contracts. We earn your business every visit. If you're happy with our work, you'll keep calling us — that's our only "contract."

The price we quote is the price you pay. No fuel surcharges, no equipment fees, no surprise add-ons. If additional work comes up during a visit, we'll always ask before doing it.

Recurring customers who schedule weekly or biweekly service typically pay less per visit than one-time jobs. Call us for a quote and we'll give you both options.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does lawn mowing cost in Alabama?

In North Jefferson County, most residential lawn mowing runs $35-$75 per visit depending on lot size, terrain, and obstacles. The average home in Warrior or Gardendale is in the $40-$55 range. We provide free estimates so you know exactly what you'll pay.

Do you offer discounts for recurring service?

Yes. Customers on a regular weekly or biweekly schedule typically pay a lower per-visit rate than one-time or occasional jobs. It's better for your lawn, and it helps us plan our routes more efficiently — so we pass that savings on to you.

What's included in a standard mow?

Every standard mowing visit includes mowing, edging along driveways and sidewalks, trimming around fences and obstacles, and blowing off hard surfaces. Your property looks clean and professional when we leave — every time.

How do I get an exact price for my yard?

Call or text us at 205-306-0980 or request a quote online. We'll come take a look at your property and give you an exact price — no obligation, no pressure.
