SMS Consent & Opt-Out

How we use text messages to keep you in the loop on your lawn service.

What we send

If you're a Lindsey Lawn Plus customer, we send a small number of service-related text messages tied to your appointments and billing. These are transactional — not marketing or promotional.

  • Appointment reminder — the day before your scheduled service, so you know when to expect us.
  • On-my-way — when our crew is heading to your property, so you don't get surprised.
  • Job complete — a quick confirmation when we finish, in case you have questions.
  • Invoice ready — when your bill is available to view and pay.

That's it. We don't send sales pitches, special offers, or messages about anything other than your active service with us.

How you opt in

You give us permission to text you when you become a customer. That happens one of three ways:

  • You agree verbally during your initial quote or onboarding conversation.
  • You provide your phone number on our quote-request form and confirm you'd like service updates by text.
  • You sign a service agreement that includes consent to text-based notifications.

Standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply. Lindsey Lawn Plus does not charge for these messages — only your carrier might, depending on your plan.

How to opt out

Reply STOP to any text message you receive from us. That immediately removes you from all future text notifications. You'll get a single confirmation text and then nothing else.

You can also call or text 205-306-0980 any time and ask us to stop, or email lindseylawnplus@gmail.com with "Stop texts" in the subject line. Opting out of texts does not cancel your service — it just changes how we communicate. We'll still reach you by phone or email.

If you ever want to opt back in after stopping, reply START to our number or let us know directly.

For help

Reply HELP to any of our texts and you'll get a short message with our phone number and a reminder of how to reach us. You can also call 205-306-0980 during business hours.

Privacy

We use your phone number only to send the service-related messages described above. We do not sell, rent, or share customer phone numbers with third parties for marketing purposes. Messages are sent through Twilio, our SMS service provider, and are subject to their security and privacy practices.

Frequency varies based on your service schedule. A weekly customer might get four to six messages a month; a one-time customer might get two or three total.

Questions?

Call or text 205-306-0980, or email lindseylawnplus@gmail.com. We're a small local business and a real person will get back to you.
