The Finishing Touch That Makes the Difference

Edging is what separates a good-looking lawn from a great one. Clean, crisp lines along your driveway, sidewalks, and garden beds give your property a manicured, professional appearance that mowing alone can't achieve.

At Lindsey Lawn Plus, edging is included with every mowing visit — it's not an add-on, it's standard. We also offer standalone edging and trimming for customers who handle their own mowing but want that finished look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is edging included with your mowing service?

Yes. Every mowing visit from Lindsey Lawn Plus includes edging along driveways and sidewalks, plus string trimming around fences, trees, and obstacles. It’s not an add-on — it’s standard.

Can I get edging without mowing?

Yes. We offer standalone edging and trimming for customers who mow their own lawn but want professional edges. Call 205-306-0980 for pricing.

How often should lawns be edged?

We edge every time we mow — typically weekly or biweekly. Consistent edging prevents grass from creeping over sidewalks and driveways and keeps your property looking sharp year-round.
