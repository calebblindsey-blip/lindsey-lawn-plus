About Lindsey Lawn Plus

Locally Owned. Family Operated.

Lindsey Lawn Plus is owned and operated by Caleb and Amber Lindsey, right here in Warrior, Alabama. We started this business because we believe every homeowner deserves a lawn care crew that shows up when they say they will, does what they say they'll do, and treats your property like their own.

We're not a franchise. We're not a national chain. We're a local family that lives and works in the same community we serve. When you call Lindsey Lawn Plus, you're talking to the people who actually mow your lawn.

Why We Started

We saw a gap in our area. Homeowners were either hiring unreliable crews that skipped visits, or paying big-company prices for impersonal service. We knew we could do better — show up consistently, communicate clearly, and do quality work at a fair price.

That's the foundation of Lindsey Lawn Plus: reliability, quality, and honest pricing. No contracts, no hidden fees, no excuses. We earn your business every single visit.
