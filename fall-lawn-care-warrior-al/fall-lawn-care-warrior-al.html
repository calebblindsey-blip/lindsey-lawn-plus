Why Fall Care Matters

A clean lawn going into winter means less disease, fewer pests, and faster recovery in spring. Leaves left on the lawn over winter create a wet, matted layer that kills the grass underneath. By the time you notice the damage in March, you've lost months of recovery time.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I schedule fall lawn care in Warrior?

Leaf fall peaks from late October through December in the Warrior area. We recommend at least one cleanup in November. Properties with heavy tree coverage may need multiple visits throughout the season.

Does fall cleanup include leaf removal?

Yes. Full leaf removal is our primary fall service. We clear leaves from your lawn, beds, and hard surfaces. Everything is bagged and hauled away.

How does fall care help my lawn in spring?

Removing leaves and debris before winter prevents smothering, reduces fungal disease, and allows your grass to enter dormancy healthy. A clean lawn in November means faster, fuller green-up in March.
