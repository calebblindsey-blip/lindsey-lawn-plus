Don't Let Leaves Smother Your Lawn

When fall hits North Alabama, leaves pile up fast. Oaks, maples, and sweetgums drop thousands of leaves across your yard from October through December. Left alone, those leaves smother your grass, trap moisture, invite pests, and create a breeding ground for fungal disease.

Lindsey Lawn Plus handles complete leaf removal — we rake, blow, and haul away every last leaf so your yard stays healthy and clean through winter.

Why Leaf Removal Matters

🌿

Protects Your Grass

Thick leaf cover blocks sunlight and traps moisture, killing the grass underneath. Removing leaves lets your lawn breathe.

🐛

Prevents Pests

Wet, decomposing leaves attract insects, rodents, and create conditions for lawn fungus and disease.

🏠

Curb Appeal

A yard covered in leaves looks neglected. Clean it up and your property immediately looks maintained and cared for.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I get leaves removed?

In North Alabama, leaf fall peaks from late October through December. We recommend at least one thorough cleanup in November and another in December or January. Properties with heavy tree coverage may benefit from weekly or biweekly removal during peak season.

Do you haul the leaves away?

Yes. We bag all leaves and haul them away from your property. You don’t need to worry about disposal.

How much does leaf removal cost?
